Where does the 10% bonus come from?

The bonus 10% will be given via the buyback contract that will repay your Full Send tokens dollar for dollar + 10%. The 10% acts as a reward for holders to give them an extra incentive to keep holding. This will not affect the token value for Arcadix because the same amount of liquidity will be deposited. Trading will open up at where Full Send ended, so prices may be slightly different but won't be drastically affected. If Full Send ended trading at 200B per BNB, then Arcadix will open at 2M per BNB, thus being the same price.

Will BNB rewards act as a sell on the token?

The team has found a way to avoid this coding error that sells liquidity to fund the rewards program. This was an error many tokens went through at beginning stages of BNB rewards, but this will not happen with Arcadix as we figured out a way to take tax before it hits liquidity. This will act as a pool, hence the BNB pool. If a 100 BNB buy comes in, 6% of that transaction tax will be filtered out and sent to the BNB pool BEFORE it hits the liquidity pool. Due to this, the rewards will not show up as a sell against liquidity.

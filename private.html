Why Arcadix?

Arcadix represents a global cryptocurrency community and a comprehensive vision for a decentralized ecosystem of apps to provide utility to token holders.

Core Vision

As a decentralized monetary system, cryptocurrency is inherently about bringing the common people together. The mission of Arcadix is to give cryptocurrency users around the world their very own platform to facilitate this community.

Arcadix Team

Our team has a plethora of experience in the software, application development, marketing, and design industries. The Arcadix team is dedicated to bridging the gap between non-crypto users and the cryptoverse.

Community

Arcadix has more than 10,000 active users on Telegram, Discord, Twitter and Reddit. The Arcadix family is adopting new active members every day while aiming to build a sustainable future for its token ecosystem.
